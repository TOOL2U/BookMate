### Regarding Guideline 3.2 - Business Model

**BookMate is intended for the GENERAL PUBLIC.** Here are answers to your questions:

**1. Is your app restricted to users who are part of a single company?**

**No.** BookMate is available to anyone who owns or manages rental properties. Our target users include:
- Individual property owners and landlords
- Small to large property management companies
- Real estate investors
- Anyone managing rental income and expenses

There is no company restriction. Anyone can download and use the app.

**2. Is your app designed for use by a limited or specific group of companies?**

**No.** BookMate is designed for the general public rental property market. Any individual or business can use the app without restriction. There is no:
- Company verification required
- Partnership agreement needed
- Whitelist of approved organizations
- Limitation on who can register

**3. What features in the app, if any, are intended for use by the general public?**

**ALL features are for public use:**
- **Balance Tracking** - Track property-specific account balances
- **Income & Expense Management** - Record rental income and property expenses
- **Profit & Loss Reports** - Generate P&L statements for properties
- **Financial Analytics** - View charts and insights for property performance
- **Google Sheets Integration** - Sync data with Google Sheets for external reporting
- **Multi-Property Management** - Manage multiple rental properties in one place

These features serve any property owner or manager, regardless of their organization.

**4. How do users obtain an account?**

**Public Registration - No Approval Required:**

Users can create an account by:
1. Downloading BookMate from the App Store
2. Opening the app and tapping "Sign Up"
3. Registering with:
   - Email and password, OR
   - Google Sign-In (OAuth)

**No business verification, approval process, or invitation is required.** Anyone can immediately create an account and start using the app.
